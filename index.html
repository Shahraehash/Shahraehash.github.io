Comparing Temporal Change of DNA Methylation Between Type 1 Diabetes Patients and Healthy Individuals
November-December 2021

We trained three Markov Models to model the change of methylation sites with respect to age in individuals with type 1 diabetes and healthy individuals. We prune and structurally compare Markov Models to identify distinct patterns of methylation in healthy individuals and those with type 1 diabetes. Moreover, we show these Markov Models are generalizable by using them to predict age given unseen methylation sites and benchmark their performance against a linear regression baseline and deep neural network.
